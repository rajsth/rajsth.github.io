About Babaal patro

Babaal patro (बबाल पात्रो) is a free, open-source Nepali calendar app built with Flutter. It uses the Bikram Sambat (BS) calendar system used in Nepal and displays dates in Devanagari numerals with full Nepali language support.

  • Calendar — Monthly BS calendar grid with today highlighted, daily tithi (तिथि) and panchang (पञ्चाङ्ग) information, and Nepali public holidays.
  • Reminders — Create reminders tied to BS dates with BS-aware monthly and yearly recurrence. Supports 10 categories including Birthday, Anniversary, Healthcare, and more.
  • Date Converter — Convert dates between Bikram Sambat (BS) and Gregorian (AD) calendars.
  • Home Screen Widget — See today's Nepali date on your Android or iOS home screen without opening the app.
  • Themes — Dark and light modes with 8 accent color presets.

Built for Nepali communities worldwide who use the Bikram Sambat calendar for daily life, festivals, and cultural events.
